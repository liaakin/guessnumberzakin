Rules

Guess My Number

1. Objective: Guess the secret number between 1 and 20.

2. Start the Game:

- Click "Check!" after entering your guess in the input field.

3. Feedback:

- If your guess is too high or too low, the game will give you hints.

- The background color will change if you win.

4. Score:

- You start with 20 points. Each incorrect guess reduces the score by 1.

5. Highscore:

- If you guess correctly, your score is compared to your previous best and the higher score is saved as your highscore.

6. Play Again:

- Click "Again!" to reset the game and try a new number.
